Phase 1

As a player, I want to be able to rotate items that we investigate, so that I can feel like I was actively looking and found clues.

Phase 2

As a player, I want to hear reminders or clues of the storyline, so that I can remember there is a part of a story.

As a player, when I pick things up in the missing girl's timeline, I want to hear her perspective of what was going through her mind, so we can learn her view of things.

As a player in the mad scientist's timeline, I want to see something that hints he is a mad scientist.

Phase 3

As a player, I want to hear their thoughts as we walk through the dark screen (the VR loading screen) as it is a VR experience of a simulation.
